LAUREN is a human intelligent smart home. LAUREN will visit your home, deploy a series of devices, and watch over you. LAUREN will control your home for you, attempting to be better than an AI, understanding you as a person. She will learn faster than an algorithm, adapting to your desires and anticipating your needs.

How do I get LAUREN?

You can sign up for LAUREN by clicking the "GET LAUREN" link. You will be asked to record a short video explaining why you want to try LAUREN. Lauren will follow up with you to talk with you more about it, and if you like, schedule a time to try LAUREN. The experience will take place in your own home and will last three days.

What?

LAUREN is a performance art piece by Lauren McCarthy. It is interactive, meaning you can participate by inviting Lauren into your home. The performance lasts three days.
